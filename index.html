SPANISH

Spanish is one of the most widely used languages in many circumstances, including both work and tourism. It offers the oppotunity to explore numerous Latin American countries, including the beautiful Colombia. In this presentation, I will talk about Colombia and some of its rich traditions. In Colombia, we speak Spanish, but we also mix it with many expressions from Castilian. Colombia is known for the friendliness of its people and the diversity of its accents, which vary significantly from one region to another. Additionaly the country features a rich cultures and traditions, from the vibrant music and dance of the Caribbean to the festivies and celebrations in the Andes. Bellow I will show you some of them.
